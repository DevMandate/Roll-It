How to Play Pig Game

- Each turn, a player rolls a die as many times as they wish. Each result is added to their current score.
- If they roll a 1, they lose all current score and it becomes the other player's turn.
- They can choose to "Hold" to add current score to their total score and pass the turn.
- First player to reach 100 wins.
